About HDK

HDK is a Greek producer based in London. Since signing to Roots of Society he released his debut album Sun Stroked in 2014 and most recently the full length album Old Fashioned in 2015. HDK has collaborated with Awon & Dephlow on their critically acclaimed albums Dephacation and Matte Black Soul and appeared on Cult Classic Records compilation Friends & Family 3 with the fan favourite track Cycles. Other collaborations include tracks with Numatik, Josiah the Gift, Sheik Ron, Mostafa and many more...

HDK strives to promote Hip Hop culture in its purest form.
